AGROTEXTILE

Agrotextile is a great solution for efficient production in agriculture and horticulture as well as landscaping. Special design and high quality of our Agrotextile improve its consumer properties.

Order Now!

Basic properties

  • Agrotextile - it is 100% polypropylene fiber of different density;
  • UV resistance (has a high UV stabilization);
  • resistance to environmental influences (rotting, mold and mildew effect);
  • service life - up to 5 years;
  • pass water and air.

Used

  • in nurseries of ornamental trees and shrubs, with container and soil cultivation;
  • in horticulture for growing strawberries, strawberries and other plants;
  • when disaggregated gardens, green areas and landscaping;
  • on the trading floors of plants.

Benefits Agrotextile plant nurseries

  • Agrotextile black mulching effectively inhibits the growth of weeds and eliminates the need for costly and harmful to human health herbicides;
  • the soil under the mulch layer of material in the summer perfectly retains moisture, which reduces the need for irrigation;
  • Agrotextile summer protects the soil and your crop, excessive heat, high winds, pests, protection from exposure to UV rays. In winter, it protects from severe frost and sudden hypothermia and freezing of the root system of perennial plants;
  • passes the air and water flows, and, most importantly, - liquid additives and fertilizers. Thus, plants seedlings (whether strawberries, tomatoes, sprouts, or other) get all the necessary conditions for growth, while Agrotextile prevents weeds to break through the mulch.

Technical specifications

  • grammage (density) - 100 g / m 2 , 130 g / m 2 , or upon request
  • Width - 110 cm, 180 cm, 250 cm, 320 cm, 380 cm, 420 cm or on request;
  • Packaging - standard (in rolls of 100 m);
  • Colour - standard black or at customer's request.
