AGROTEXTILE

Agrotextile is a great solution for efficient production in agriculture and horticulture as well as landscaping. Special design and high quality of our Agrotextile improve its consumer properties.

Order Now!

Basic properties

  • agrotextile - it is 100% polypropylene fabric of different density;
  • UV resistance (it has a high UV stabilization);
  • resistance to environmental influences (rotting, mold and mildew effect);
  • lifetime - over 5 years;
  • the fabric passes water and air.

Using

  • in nurseries of ornamental trees and shrubs, with container and soil cultivation;
  • in horticulture for growing strawberries, wild strawberries and other plants;
  • when laying out of gardens, green areas and landscaping;
  • on trading floors of plants.

Advantages agrotextile when using in plant nurseries

  • black mulching agrotextile effectively inhibits the growth of weeds and eliminates the need for costly and harmful to human health herbicides;
  • the soil under the layer of mulch material perfectly retains moisture in summer redusing the need for irrigation;
  • agrotextile protects the soil and plants from excessive heat, strong winds, exposure of UV rays in summer. And in winter it protects from hard frost and rapid hypothermia and freezing of the root system of perennial plants;
  • it passes the air and water flows, and, most importantly, - liquid additives and fertilizers. Thus, plants seedlings (whether strawberries, tomatoes, sprouts, or other) get all the necessary conditions for growth, while agrotextile doesn't let weeds through the mulch.

Technical specifications

  • thickness - 100 g / m 2 , 130 g / m 2 , or upon request
  • width - 110 cm, 180 cm, 250 cm, 320 cm, 380 cm, 420 cm or on request;
  • packaging - standard (in rolls of 100 m);
  • colour - standard black or at customer's request.

Polypropylene garden containers

Using containers for plants and seedlings - it is a very popular method of cultivation ornamental plants and seedlings in nurseries. These flexible containers are designed to store and transport.

Order Now!

Basic properties

  • containers made of different density polypropylene fabric;
  • resistance to ultraviolet (it has a high UV-stabilization);
  • resistance to environmental influences (rotting, mold and mildew effect);
  • lifetime - over 5 years;
  • containers passes water and air.

Advantages of growing plants in containers for nursery

  • the container is easier to carry the seedlings and work with it;
  • in contrast to containers with rigid structure, soft containers are very unpretentious, take up little space;
  • roots remain intact - it reduces the possibility of accidental death of the plants;
  • containers are in favor from buyers.

Technical specifications

Polypropylene garden containers can be of various volume, diameter and height according to the customer. Thickness - mainly 110 g / m 2 and 200 g / m 2 . Color - mainly black and white.
